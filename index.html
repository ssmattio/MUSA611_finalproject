Covid-19: Cases through Time

In January 2020, the first confirmed case of Coronavirus, or Covid-19, was confirmed in the United States. Two months later, the number of cases began to increase rapidly, causing most states to declare Shelter-in-Place policies and shut down non-essential businesses.

This application shows the number of cases by province and country. Use the slider to see how coronavirus hotspots change as time goes on.
